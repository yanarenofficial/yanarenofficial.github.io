My Roommate Killed Me and Himself, and Now We Have to Save the World Together!?

My Roommate Killed Me and Himself, and Now We Have to Save the World Together!? is an experimental light-novel-style series that blends dark comedy, apocalyptic fantasy, and emotionally volatile character dynamics.

The story follows two classmates whose deaths do not mark an ending, but a beginning: forced into a surreal post-death world where saving reality itself becomes their only path forward.

Format

The project is being developed as a multi-volume light novel series. The first volume is currently in progress.

Tone & Focus

  • Dark humor layered over emotional trauma
  • Apocalyptic stakes with intimate character conflict
  • Surreal afterlife mechanics and world-saving contracts
  • Identity, guilt, and forced cooperation

Status

The first volume is actively being written. This page will later expand with summaries, reading order, and release structure.
