My Roommate Killed Me and Himself, and Now We Have to Save the World Together!?

Light novel series · Isekai / dark comedy · Flagship project

Series Overview

My Roommate Killed Me and Himself, and Now We Have to Save the World Together!? is an isekai light-novel-style series that blends dark comedy, apocalyptic fantasy, and volatile roommate dynamics. It follows two classmates whose lives end in the worst way possible—murder-suicide— only for them to wake up in a different world with a single condition placed on their existence: work together, or watch reality collapse.

Their deaths are not an ending but a beginning. Thrown into a post-death, otherworldly setting with world-saving contracts, strange powers, and incomprehensible overseers, the pair are forced into a partnership neither of them wants, under stakes neither of them can afford to ignore.

Format & Structure

The project is being developed as a multi-volume light novel series. The first volume is currently in progress and is planned to introduce:

  • The circumstances leading up to their deaths in the original world
  • The rules and structure of the new isekai environment
  • The core “save the world or disappear” contract that binds them
  • The early stages of their forced alliance and clashing personalities

Tone & Themes

Although the premise starts from a grim event, the series leans heavily into dark humor and emotionally charged character interactions rather than pure edge.

  • Comedy emerging from impossible situations and broken people
  • Apocalyptic stakes contrasted with petty, human-level resentment
  • Surreal isekai mechanics and world-saving contracts
  • Identity, guilt, responsibility, and the question of whether either of them deserves a second chance
