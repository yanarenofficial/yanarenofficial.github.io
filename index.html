Yanaren

Rentarou Yanazaki (柳崎 蓮太郎, nicknamed Yanaren) was born in New Brunswick and raised in Roxbury, New Jersey. His native language is English, and he is also a self-taught speaker and writer of Japanese. Yanazaki discovered his passion for writing fiction in 2024, producing two original Japanese short stories that same year (A Boy Named X and Stanley). He has since expanded into English fiction and is currently working on the sci-fi short story series Alisa 2084, as well as the first volume of the light novel series My Roommate Killed Me and Himself, and Now We Have to Save the World Together!?

Yanazaki holds a BA in East Asian Languages & Cultures with a minor in Creative Writing from Rutgers University, and he previously studied for one year at Fukui University as an exchange student in Japan.

Other accomplishments include completing an intensive 100,000-word Japanese writing challenge in three months and achieving JLPT N1 certification at age nineteen.
