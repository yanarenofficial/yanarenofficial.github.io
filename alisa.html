Alisa 2084

Alisa 2084 is an ongoing science-fiction flash fiction series centered on Alisa, a reckless, hyper-capable operative who moves across time, space, and parallel worlds in pursuit of high-risk contracts that tend to unravel far more than intended.

Each story functions as a self-contained mission, but over time the series gradually exposes Alisa’s personal history, fractured family ties, and the psychological cost of treating reality as something disposable.

Format

The series is written as episodic flash fiction. Individual entries range from intense action sequences to quiet, emotionally charged character studies.

Tone & Focus

  • High-speed action and spectacle
  • Time travel and alternate histories
  • Dark humor layered over psychological weight
  • Identity, regret, and consequence

Status

The Alisa 2084 series is an active, long-term project. New episodes are written intermittently and may later be curated into one or more collected volumes.
