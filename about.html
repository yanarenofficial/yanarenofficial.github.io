About Yanaren

Yanaren is the pen name of Rentarou Yanazaki（柳崎 蓮太郎）, a bilingual fiction writer working in both English and Japanese. This site serves as a personal publishing platform for original stories, experimental series, and long-form narrative projects.

Rather than focusing on a single genre, Yanaren’s work explores a wide range of tones and structures: science fiction, psychological fiction, dark humor, surrealism, and introspective character-driven stories. Many projects are released as serial or episodic works.

This site is intentionally minimal. It exists primarily as a quiet studio space for development rather than a polished commercial storefront. Projects are built slowly, refined over time, and released when they are ready.

Languages & Approach

Yanaren writes in both English and Japanese. The Japanese works hosted here are original literary projects rather than translations. Language itself is treated not only as a tool but as a creative constraint that shapes structure, rhythm, and perspective.

Long-Term Vision

The long-term goal of this platform is to function as a self-contained creative archive: a place where multiple narrative universes, standalone stories, and experimental projects can coexist without algorithm pressure or platform dependency.

Nothing here is built for speed. Everything is built for endurance.
